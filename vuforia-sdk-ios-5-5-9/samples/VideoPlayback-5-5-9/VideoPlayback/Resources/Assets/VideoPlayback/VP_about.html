Welcome to 700 Level!

Scan the image target and play the video on top of it.

Key Functionality

  • Play video on target for devices that support (devices that don't support will play full screen)
  • Optionally play video full screen in native player

Targets

Stones and Chips

Instructions

  • Point camera at target to view
  • Tap video to play
  • Double tap to access options menu
